RageCage

Ever send an angry email, facebook message or youtube comment that you later regretted?

We created RageCage at CalHacks 5.0 to help combat this. RageCage is a Chrome extension that uses Machine Learning to detect how angry your message is, before you send it. If your message contains enough anger, the extension will remind you to cool off via a pop-up on your screen.

As the saying goes, "speak when you are angry and you will make the best speech you will ever regret."
